About Adonis Decorating

Adonis Decorating is a trusted, family-run painting and decorating company owned and operated by Alex Adonis Matthews. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we specialise in high-quality interior painting, exterior painting, wallpapering, plastering repairs and beautiful decorative finishes for both residential and commercial properties.

We proudly serve clients throughout South West London and all surrounding areas in the UK, as well as Mullingar and the surrounding regions in Ireland. Our reputation is built on reliability, meticulous attention to detail, clean and tidy work, honest pricing, and complete customer satisfaction.
