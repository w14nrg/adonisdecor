About Adonis Decorating

Adonis Decorating is owned and run by Alex Adonis Matthews, a time-served professional painter and decorator with over 15 years of hands-on experience. Alex personally oversees every job, ensuring the highest standards of workmanship, clean and tidy sites, and complete customer satisfaction.

We specialise in premium interior painting, exterior painting, wallpapering, decorative finishes and ongoing property maintenance. Whether it’s a full house refresh in South West London, a new colour scheme in West London, or protecting your home from the Irish weather in and around Mullingar, you can trust us to deliver flawless results every time.

Our reputation is built on reliability, honest pricing with no hidden costs, and the personal touch that only a family-run business can offer.
