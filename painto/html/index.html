About Adonis Decorating

Adonis Decorating is owned and personally run by Alex Adonis Matthews. With over 15 years of hands-on experience as a time-served painter and decorator, Alex takes care of every job from start to finish.

We specialise in high-quality interior painting, exterior painting, wallpapering, decorative finishes and property maintenance. We work throughout South West London, West London and in & around Mullingar, Ireland.

Our promise is simple: clean, tidy work, honest prices and results you will be proud of.
